Ph.D. Research Area

My main area of research is Software Engineering for Artificial Intelligence (SE4AI) and concerns optimizing non-functional requirements (NFRs) of machine learning systems. I am focused on identifying and optimizing NFRs to improve the overall quality of artificial intelligence systems. My primary focus is optimizing Ml systems' Sustainability to improve the Society, Environment and Economy aspects.
